How To Use

  1. Choose One-Way or Round Trip.
  2. Select your starting location from the dropdown.
  3. Enter the surveillance event address.
  4. Tap Open In Google Maps to launch directions.
  5. Copy the one-way mileage, hour(s) and minute(s) into the fields.
  6. Tap Calculate Billing.
  7. Enter the returned values in TrackOps Mobile.
